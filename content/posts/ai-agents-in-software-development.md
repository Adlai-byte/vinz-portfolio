## Abstract

AI agents are rapidly changing software development workflows. This article explores current capabilities, limitations, and future directions.

## Key Findings

1. **Code Generation** — AI can generate boilerplate and routine code with high accuracy
2. **Debugging** — Agents can identify and fix common bugs autonomously
3. **Architecture** — AI assistants help explore design trade-offs faster

## Conclusion

AI agents are best used as collaborative tools that augment developer capabilities rather than replace them.
